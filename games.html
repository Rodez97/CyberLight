Calm: A Puzzle Experience

Calm: A Puzzle Experience is designed to challenge your mind while you feel immersed in the relaxing and beautiful sounds of three themes: piano, sea, and rain. You decide the player you are gonna be, the victory seeker or the contemplator of the journey, it is up to you. There are a total of three hundred and seventy beautiful puzzles waiting to be unraveled by the brightest minds out there, are you one of them?
